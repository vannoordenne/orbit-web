or.bit

Observatory for Research in Breakthrough and Interactive Technologies

or.bit is a practice-based research collective that investigates, questions and makes technology tangible. We work at the intersection of research, design, experimentation and education.

From a transdisciplinary viewpoint, we connect critical reflection to concrete forms: publications, methods, workshops, installations, provocatypes and tools.

Three branches

Research

Investigating the relationship between technology and society through publications, frameworks and transferable methods.

Lab

Making technology tangible through provocatypes, installations and interactive experiments.

Education

Developing workshops, trainings and learning formats that help people engage critically with technology.

or.bit focuses on emerging and interactive technologies, with particular attention to AI, digital systems, infrastructures, interfaces and their societal implications.
