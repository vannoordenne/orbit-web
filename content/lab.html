Lab

In the Lab we make technology tangible and experiential. We build provocatypes, installations, interactive experiments and other investigative prototypes that make complex systems feelable and discussable.

The Lab is where thinking becomes material — and where material asks new questions.

What is a provocatype?

A provocatype is a prototype designed not to be used, but to provoke. It makes a system visible, a consequence tangible, an assumption discussable. It is research made physical.
