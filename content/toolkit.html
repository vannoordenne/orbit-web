Dark Tech Toolkit

PROTOTYPE IN DEVELOPMENT

The Dark Tech Toolkit is a physical toolkit for critically investigating technology. It helps users make digital systems, AI and technological infrastructures discussable, tangible and analysable.

The toolkit is designed as a hands-on instrument — something you can pick up, spread on a table and use to think together. Not a manual. Not a textbook. A set of tools.

Contents (provisional)

■ Analysis cards for mapping technological systems
■ Discussion formats and facilitation guides
■ Speculation prompts and scenario exercises
■ Ethical matrices and decision frameworks
■ Case study materials
■ Reference sheets and concept overviews

Who is it for?

■ Educators and workshop facilitators
■ Students in technology, design and social sciences
■ Organisations wanting to think critically about their technology use
■ Researchers and curious practitioners
